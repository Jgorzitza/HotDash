# CX Escalations Modal (Offline Description)

- Initial focus: Close button (`button[aria-label="Close escalation modal"]`).
- Tab order: close → suggested reply textarea → internal note textarea → Approve & send → Escalate → Mark resolved → Cancel.
- Support inbox reminder: internal note helper text instructs operators to email customer.support@hotrodan.com within 5 minutes if follow-up is required.
- Conversation history uses `role="log"` with polite announcements and visible Agent/Customer labels.
- Toast expectation: `Reply sent to {customer}` on success, error toast instructs retry before escalating to reliability.
